# Research

This folder is the strategy brain of the repo.

If you want to understand Professor Curious properly before making ads, start here.

## What lives here

### `research/product/`
Big-picture understanding of the product:
- what it really does
- what problem it solves
- what kinds of users it serves
- what ad angles are strongest

Most important files:
- `PROF_CURIOUS_PRODUCT_DOSSIER_2026-03-12.md`
- `PROF_CURIOUS_CREATIVE_RESEARCH_V2_2026-03-12.md`

### `research/user-feedback/`
Direct user signal:
- raw survey export
- distilled notes
- question list
- recurring insights

Most important files:
- `prof-curious-user-feedback-2026-03-08.csv`
- `USER_RESEARCH_QUESTIONS.md`
- `INSIGHTS_2026-03-12.md`

## How to use this folder

Use this folder when you want to answer:
- what should the ad actually say?
- what do users care about most?
- what do they complain about?
- what promises feel true?
- what kind of emotional storytelling fits the product?

## Best reading order for a non-technical person

1. `research/product/PROF_CURIOUS_CREATIVE_RESEARCH_V2_2026-03-12.md`
2. `research/product/PROF_CURIOUS_PRODUCT_DOSSIER_2026-03-12.md`
3. `research/user-feedback/INSIGHTS_2026-03-12.md`
4. `research/user-feedback/USER_RESEARCH_QUESTIONS.md`
