- simple front selfie camera
- plain room background
- face is the main content
- very small natural movement only

- raw, casual, unpolished

Indian man in his early 20s sitting in a simple room at night. Plain wall, ceiling fan visible, open door in background, simple chair, ordinary t-shirt. Nothing aesthetic or designed. Looks like real everyday room footage.

Camera:
- front camera is already on

Important:
