# Doubt Mazak Street Interview

India UGC experiment.

Core truth:
- students stop asking doubts because classmates mock them

Why this format:
- multiple faces are expected
- character consistency is not required
- high emotional truth
- strong fit for Professor Curious as a private, non-judgmental helper

Recommended generation style:
- Sora or Veo
- street interview / school gate realism
- 6 x 8 second clips + locked India outro

Structure:
1. host hook
2. student says kids laugh
3. student says they stopped asking
4. student says teachers move on
5. student says Professor Curious helps privately
6. student says confidence improves
7. locked outro
